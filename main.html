What is punycode?

Punycode is a way of representing urls that allows more characters than ASCII.

Why is this a problem?

Someone can suplant the identity of a oficial website and send a link to the fake website. A person can't diferenciate by the url the original from the fake and this can end in something dangerous for your privacy and/or data.
